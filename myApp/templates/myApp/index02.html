Overall work flow

In this step, you will indicate location of your target communimty. This is important to assess food availability, which is largely depending on natural environment

Based on the information from your previous selection, app will show you a list of food item potentially available in your target area. You will then, discuss with local people about seasonal availability of each food item as well as accesibility (self production or market) This is important step as many of nutritious food have strong seasonality and also, it may be only available from the market, which will give financial burden to local vulnerable.

In this step, you will identify beneficiaries for your activity under three category. (1) indivisual target, (2) household target, (3) entire community

In this step, you will guide target benefitiaries to optimize combination of foods available locally, based on the key nutrition requirement.

In case your target benefitiary fail to satisfy all the nutrition requirement, you can discuss further for introduction of new crops not produced in respective area. in this section, you can conduct feasibility assessment of new crops potentially introduced in the area

In this step, you add information on seasonal availability of newly introduced crop, just like step 2.

In this step, you will review and update food item combination making use of new food introduced in the area.
