Objective
NFA tool is designed to support health worker, agricultural extension worker, volunteer, NGO, plus any other group of people who works on nutrition improvement.
NFA tool try to provide better dietary guidance to local people. (nutritious food item, available and affordable for local people)
Scientific evidence
NFA tool is designed to apply science based information for dietary guidance, (1)Daily nutrition recommendatioon (WHO, FAO) and (2) Nutrition contents in each food item (FAO)
Availability and affordability
through dialogue with local community and crop suitability information, NFA tool will identify locally available and affordable food item
Linkage: health and agriculture
NFA tool will link health worker and agriculture worker through identifying locally suitable nutrition rich food with required quantity
